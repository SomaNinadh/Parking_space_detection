Why do I need to add my vehicle number at the time of checking availability? Is it safe?

Your vehicle number helps us provide you with accurate and personalized parking space availability. It allows the system to identify your vehicle and notify you when a spot is available. Rest assured, your vehicle number is securely encrypted and stored in compliance with data protection laws.

How can your platform help me? How safe is your platform?

Our platform helps you find available parking spaces quickly and conveniently. You can view real-time availability.. Safety and security are our top priorities. We use advanced encryption and security measures to protect your data and ensure a safe user experience.

Does ParkEasy support cashless payment?

Yes, ParkEasy supports cashless payment options for parking reservations and fees. You can easily pay through the app using credit/debit cards or mobile payment methods for a seamless parking experience.

How and when to make the payment?

Payments for parking reservations can be made directly through the app when you exit the parking lot. You'll be prompted to enter your preferred payment method, and the payment will be processed securely. For regular parking fees, payment can be made upon arrival at the parking location, either through the app or at designated payment kiosks.
